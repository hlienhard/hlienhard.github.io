Visualizing Time

Sketch 1

"This clock displays each numeral of time as a shape, where the number of sides in the shape is equal to the numberal associated with is. For example, if it's 3:45, the clock would display a triangle, square and a pentagon. When there is a 0 in the time, there is a blank space."

Sketch 2

"Only the hour of the time is represented in this clock. Each hour of the day is associated with one step in the folding an origami paper crane. Each hour one fold is made, until 12:00 when a full crane is visible. At 1:00, it resets and just a square of paper is visible"

Sketch 3

"This clock displays time in terms of the amount of foam left in a beer. The foam dissipates as time passes, so by the end of a time period there is no foam left. The first glass represents the hour, and the last two are the digits of minutes. In this example, the clock might read 2:49."
